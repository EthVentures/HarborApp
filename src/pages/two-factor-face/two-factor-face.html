Two-Face Authentication Authentication

Two-Face Authentication is used to verify the identity of the account holder. In addition to verification, it searches to possible new relatives that have been entered into the SafeHarbour project. As a sovereign citizen, you reserve the right to reject facial scan upon login. To do so, cancel and alert a camp worker who will verify your identity the traditional way. To proceed with the Two-Face Authentication, please press continue and take a picture of your face so that the four corners match the four corners of the screen.
