Project Safe Harbor
Self Sovereign Identity
Providing high tech solutions for the millions of asylum-seekers around the world.
Biometrics & A.I.
From securing the platform to helping reconnect families, Harbor’s AI utilizes open source facial recognition frameworks to streamline the reunification process.
Direct Opportunities
Harbor’s goal is to get short-term employment opportunities in front of refugees within days of arrival. Each successful task is added as an attestation to their digital identity.
